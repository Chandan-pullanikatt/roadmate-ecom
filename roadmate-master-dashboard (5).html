Revenue Models
Define every revenue event, its charge amount, and exact partner share % for each tier
Each model defines the fee charged when that revenue event happens (e.g. a shop lists = ₹5,000). The share % determines how much each partner tier earns from that fee. For example: Regional 40% of ₹5,000 = ₹2,000 goes to the regional partner in whose area the shop is located.
